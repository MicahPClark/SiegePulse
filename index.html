How to Play

Siege Pulse is a turn-based strategy game where you control units to capture the central tower and defeat your opponent.

Game Rules:

  • Objective: Control the central tower for 2 consecutive turns or eliminate all enemy units.
  • Movement: Click on your unit, then click on an adjacent empty square to move.
  • Tower Control: Have a unit adjacent to the tower without enemy units nearby to gain control.
●

Tip: Press 'R' to restart the game after a win.
